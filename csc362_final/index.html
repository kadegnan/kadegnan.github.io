By the Numbers: Who Dominates Olympic Track & Field?

This project visualizes the top 5 countries with the most Olympic medals in track and field using a grouped bar chart and a collapsible tree. The grouped bar chart shows the distribution of medals across different years, while the collapsible tree provides a hierarchical view of the medal winners, categorized by country, year, event, and athlete.

👋 Welcome! Here's how to explore the data

📊 Grouped Bar Chart (left)

Hover over any bar to see that country's total medal count for that year.

Hover over a color in the legend to highlight that country across all years.

Click on a year label on the left axis to explore that year's medals in the tree.

Collapsible Tree (right)

Click on any circle to expand it and see the next level of detail.

Start with a country → expand to see events → expand again to see athletes and their medals.

Click the same circle again to collapse it.
